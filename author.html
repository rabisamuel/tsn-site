The Author

Rabi Samuel

Novelist

Rabi Samuel is an Indian-Canadian author and storyteller with a deep fascination for the places where myth and technology quietly collide.

A programmer since 1986, he has spent a career watching machines do things their designers never intended. The Sixteenth Night grew from that astonishment, and from a Naadi reading he once sat through himself, in a small room where a stranger read his life back to him from a palm leaf.

His debut novel, Cold Mine, a literary thriller set in the Kolar Gold Fields where he was born, was published in 2026. The Sixteenth Night is his second novel.

He lives in Ottawa, Ontario, with his family.
